Cryptographic Hash Digests

Thu 23 July 2015
By Kieran Hunt

If you're anything like me then you'll love a good cryptographic hash digest. A great rust library makes creating digests super easy. Rust-crypto is terrific.


let to_be_hashed: &[u8] = &[0x0, 0x1, 0x2];

let mut md5 = Md5::new();

md5.input(to_be_hashed);

let mut hashed = md5.result_str();

md5.reset();


And as simply as that you've got a hash digest of whatever you want. There are a large number of digest functions to choose from too.

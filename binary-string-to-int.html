Converting a String of Binary to an Integer

Thu 17 July 2014
By Kieran Hunt

Earlier in the week I needed to take four u8s assembled in a Vec<u8> and combine them to form a u32. The tricky part was that the needed to be concatenated and then converted in binary form.

Documentation seems a little scarce on the topic and what I could find either had to cast an integer represented in decimal or no longer existed. I'm looking at you std::num::from_str_radix. I leapt onto the IRC to ask there.

Turns out that from_str_radix wasn't that far off and it seems to have been refactored to another place in the codebase. Without further ado:

u32::from_str_radix(somestr, 2)

Proudly powered by Pelican, which takes great advantage of Python.
